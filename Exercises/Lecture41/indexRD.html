My Story

A few years ago...I started to collect a few items of Rae Dunn. Although I did not know what Rae Dunn was at the time. I bought the coffee and tea canisters from Homesense because I loved the simple design. A creamy white base with simple Black CAPITAL LETTERS.

It sat on my counter for years...before I was introduced to the fact that I too had Rae Dunn.

A friend was over for a BBQ and she pointed it out.

My friend has over 100 pieces of Rae Dunn she has collected....from mugs, to plates and bowls, as well as canisters and crocks.
